A FEW WORDS ABOUT CONNECT ACADEMY

We aim to bring together the power of an academic network to every day academic needs. Our mission is to create an academic network that connects scholars worldwide, while ensuring reliability of their identity and of the institution they attend. An increased number of online course platforms proves that there is an obvious need to use the power of the web for community learning. Something however, is going wrong. While this number is increasing, the effort of the average student to use these platforms, is also increasing. That is because there is no single platform that is the default choice of all professors and all students. We are many, but we are in different platforms.

These platforms, being different in scope, have failed to become part of the student's everyday academic life. These platforms are not integrated with a student's real courses and real needs. The default learning management systems universities use link to nothing. Conenct Academy solves that

MAKING EDUCATION SIMPLE

Education is already complicated enough; One has to find the right approach to it, the right time, the right institution, the right people and the right country. If you are a student, Connect Academy will accompany you from being registered to a course, to being assigned homework, to submitting it, to always keep track of your time and progress. If you are a professor, we provide you the simplicity you have been longing for to manage your classes and monitor performance effortlessly
